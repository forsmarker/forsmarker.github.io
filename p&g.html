We made the very thing that makes fragrance selection such a frustration and turned it into something enjoyable. Turning it on its head. Accessible to anyone, while still maintaining a sense of exclusivity. A guided journey which helps one indulge in the finer process of discovering your true scent.

It all starts with an invitation.

They log onto the Scent Salon platform to add your details, such as fragrance “history” , which is then used to formulate your unique profile. This special someone has selected you for a unique and curated evening of the P&G premium scents, wine, and some lasting memories. At a luxe venue, a P&G brand ambassador guides you through a selection of fragrances, hand-picked to suit your preferences. These selections are based on the considerations and input from your loved one, incorporating data about your current choices in fragrance. you also get the chance to customize your bottle with your own distinctive mark. Leave the room with a feeling of satisfaction, gratitude, indulgence, and a bottle encompassing your personal grace. Something that is truly, and uniquely you.

How do you make sense- of making scents?
