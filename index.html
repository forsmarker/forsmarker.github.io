An ux experience based on a lot of content

This is one of the biggest fishing companies that makes lures. They wanted a personalised app for their customers, a place were they can collect all their social media and give away great tips & tricks while the user is out and fishing.

Through this project the team I worked with really needed to work with alot of tools that Hyper Island have provided us, like user research, prototyping, ideations and point of departure.

Our clients wanted a lot of different things in the app, so we had to scale down as much as we could and then try to resolve the layout in the app so that it became as smooth and easy for the user as we could. Sure we could have thrown in a hamburger menu but we wanted to get this app a personal and welcoming feeling.
